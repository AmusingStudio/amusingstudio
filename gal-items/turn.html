Jinx Portrait

Maxon Cinema 4D

Jinx, or "Powder" from Arcane. I was inspired by the animation in the show Arcane when it came out, and decided to draw a version of the character mimicking my own style. I wanted to find a medium ground between the style featured in the show that made it so unique, and my own style.
