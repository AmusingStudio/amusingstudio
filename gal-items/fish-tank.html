Fish Tank Simulation

Digital/Krita/Adobe AfterEffects

This was the final project for my first animation class. The characters were pretty rudimentary, as i wanted to give it a motion-graphics cutout feel. I drew inspiration from the company Supergiant games Transistor and Pyre. The process of taking each layer and animating it in Aftereffect felt similar to doing the stop motion animation I did as a kid, so I didn't mind the work it took at all.
