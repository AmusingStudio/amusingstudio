Bob

Digital/Krita/Pen Tablet

I designed this character as a part of my webcomic “Amourous”. The design was meant to be simple, and cute. I chose not to use a mouth as a sort of distinctive style, instead focusing on the overall body language and cartoon expression. The color palette was meant to be somewhat traditional princess, as I’m poking fun at the stereotypical roles of characters in the fantasy genre.
