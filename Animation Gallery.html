Gallery

I have compiled a collection of my ongoing Animation work here. I have work going back through the last several years, and it will be updated as I complete and format more material. I produce new work over time, and am currently working on updating the work visible on the site, so if you would like to see more it is most likely available upon request.

If you like what you see, my contact information is located at the bottom of every page: Feel free to drop a line! Whether it be to discuss freelancing work or just to discuss style and experiences, I would love to hear from you.

Gavin the Clown

Gavin the clown was creepy for me, even as I modeled him. I have here the images of him with texture, and you can find his turnaround in the show reel video above.

Claude

Claude and cerule are featured in my illustration Gallery as designs. Theyre very simple, but the style was very fun to sculpt!

Hades and Persephone

This, while not 3D, showcases an animatic type storyboard presentation. I wanted to give it a motion-graphics cutout feel. I drew inspiration from the company Supergiant games Transistor and Pyre. This style would be ideal for presentations.

Fish Tank Sim

An Aquarium made in Cinema 4D. The tank is sparse because I was really focused on making the glass and water believeable. The fish are textured as a school, and there is a towel showing cloth dynamics. It looks like its seen better days!

Pink Moths

Another School project, focused on randomizing school or flock behavior. This animation was done entirely with programmin in Cinema 4D's innate particle system, X particles.

Chickens! A Feathered Tail

I created this film with my own experience at heart. I have a baby sister 19 years younger than me, and she was quite the surprise. At first, I was simply excited about the cute new baby that was coming into my life- but I very quickly realized that having a tiny human around was a lot of work. This short film is about just that: Responsibility.

COMING SOON: Poppy

A lighthearted children-friendly character. She loves desserts, coffee, and curling up with a good book. She is currently being rigged, and will have a turnaround and animations soon!

COMING SOON: Slimette

Your average level one slime...but undercover. This slime is wearing a frog hat in hopes that the passing adventurers think that she is a higher level enemy.
