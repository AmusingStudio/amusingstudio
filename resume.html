About

My name is Lynette Muse and I am a 3D Animator with a focus in Character Rigging. I am practiced in Modeling, Texturing, Rigging, Lighting, and animating 3D characters, and have created a short film from scratch using these techniques, so I am very familiar with the process involved in realizing a model or shot. I also can create viable storyboards and animatics to support the previsualization process and am adept and experienced with working as part of a team. I am currently refining the film as well as working to create a library of assets for use in a children’s series in previsualization stage in my free time.
